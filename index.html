Features

Everything you need mid-semester.

Designed around real Canvas courses — weighted categories, what-ifs, and quiet grade alerts.

Weighted categories

See how exams, homework, and labs actually stack — including zero-weight groups that don’t count toward the total.

What-if planning

Preview how a future score would move your course grade before the assignment locks in.

New grade alerts

Optional notifications when a Canvas sync picks up a newly posted or changed score.

School search

Hundreds of verified Canvas schools so you can find your campus URL without guessing.

Token-based sync

Connect with a Canvas access token you create — credentials stay under your control.

Local-first data

Courses and grades live on your device. We don’t run student accounts or sell your transcript.

Canvas

Sync your school’s Canvas — on your terms.

Unlock Pro, search your school, create a Canvas access token, paste it into GPAce, then import courses. Your school will not sync with one tap until that connection is complete.

  • Works with many Canvas LMS campuses
  • Categories and assignment scores come through after you connect
  • You can revoke the token in Canvas anytime

GPAce is not affiliated with Instructure or Canvas LMS. School availability depends on each campus setup.
